## Decisión Rápida por Caso de Uso

- Calcular 100+ rutas a la vez: [Matrix Routing API](https://developer.here.com/documentation/matrix-routing-api/dev_guide/index.html)
- Optimizar orden de entregas: [Waypoints Sequence](https://developer.here.com/documentation/routing-api/dev_guide/topics/use-cases/waypoint-sequence.html)
- Rutas para camiones: [Truck Routing](https://developer.here.com/documentation/routing-api/dev_guide/topics/use-cases/truck-routing.html)
- Gestionar 10+ vehículos: [Tour Planning API](https://developer.here.com/documentation/tour-planning-api/dev_guide/index.html)
- Analizar eficiencia de rutas: [Route Matching API](https://developer.here.com/documentation/route-matching-api/dev_guide/index.html)
- Alertas de clima: [Destination Weather](https://developer.here.com/documentation/weather-api/dev_guide/index.html)
- Costos de peaje: [Toll Cost API](https://developer.here.com/documentation/toll-cost-api/dev_guide/index.html)
- Tracking de flota: [Fleet Telematics](https://developer.here.com/documentation/fleet-telematics-api/dev_guide/index.html)
- Mapas offline: [Vector Tiles + SDK](https://developer.here.com/documentation/vector-tiles-api/dev_guide/index.html)
- Estaciones de carga EV: [EV Charge Points](https://developer.here.com/documentation/ev-charging-api/dev_guide/index.html)
